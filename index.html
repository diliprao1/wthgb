The Mission

Direction over randomness

A 3-day songwriting camp designed to connect Chicago's most promising songwriters with real opportunities.

Unlike traditional songwriting camps, participation is free. Through a competitive submission process, only 30–40 songwriters are chosen to participate. Our goal is to give Chicago songwriters a shot at making career-changing records.

Submission Below · 30–40 Writers Selected

EACH SONGWRITER RECEIVES ACCESS TO A CURATED "WHO'S LOOKING" LIST FEATURING:

  • Major artists actively seeking records
  • Creative direction and song concepts
  • Reference records and inspirations
  • Genre-specific opportunities
